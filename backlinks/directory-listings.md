## Process

1. Tier 1 all in one sitting (~30 min) — same NAP.
2. After 24h, Google `"Gold Coast Pool Builders" -site:goldcoastpoolbuilders.com.au` to confirm indexing.
3. Free upgrades — makes link do-follow on directories that offer this.
4. Reword first paragraph each listing to avoid duplicate-content flag.
5. Track in spreadsheet: directory, profile URL, login, do-follow status, date.
6. Add photos where possible — most directories allow 3-5 free photos, and the profile ranks better with them.
